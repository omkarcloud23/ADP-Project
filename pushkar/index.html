Powers

Doctor Strange commands the energy of the universe through the Mystic Arts. His abilities include teleportation, mirror dimension control, astral projection, and manipulation of time and reality. His magic stems from intense focus and ancient spells taught by The Ancient One.

Weapons

Eye of Agamotto: Grants him control over the Time Stone, allowing Strange to reverse or accelerate time events.

Cloak of Levitation: A sentient relic that lets him fly and protects him in battle.

Sling Ring: Enables instantaneous travel between dimensions and universes.

Actors

Benedict Cumberbatch masterfully portrays Doctor Strange, bringing intellect and mysticism together. Supporting characters include Wong (Benedict Wong), The Ancient One (Tilda Swinton), and Christine Palmer (Rachel McAdams).

About Dr. Stephen Strange

Early Life & Tragedy: Stephen Strange was a world-renowned neurosurgeon, known for his precision and arrogance. His life took a devastating turn after a car accident severely damaged his hands, ending his surgical career.

Journey to Kamar-Taj: Desperate for healing, Strange traveled to Nepal, where he met The Ancient One. She opened his eyes to the hidden world of mysticism and dimensions beyond reality.

Mastering the Mystic Arts: Through rigorous training and an unmatched intellect, Strange learned the secrets of spells, relics, and cosmic energy. His determination transformed him from skeptic to sorcerer.

The Sorcerer Supreme: After defending the Earth from countless threats — from Dormammu to multiversal chaos — Strange rose as the Sorcerer Supreme, protector of the Sanctum Sanctorum and the realms beyond.
